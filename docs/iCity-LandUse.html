Abstract

Developed as part of the overall iCity ontology effort, the iCity-LandUse Ontology is designed to capture concepts related to land use.

Introduction

The iCity-LandUse Ontology captures concepts related to land use and cover over time. The ontology reuses and extends the Land Based Classification Standards (LBCS) Ontology presented by (Montenegro, Gomes, Urbano, and Duarte, 2011) for this purpose.
