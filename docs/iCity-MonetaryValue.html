Introduction

Ontology to capture concepts related to monetary value. Monetary values may be attributed to things such as the purchase of a dwelling, or the salary of some Job. A Monetary Value has a dollar value relative to a particular date (year). A Monetary Value has some associated currency.

The relation between something and the point, or other geometrical thing in space, where it is. For example, the realtionship between a radio tower and a Point with a given lat and long.
