parking areac back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/icity/Parking/1.1/ParkingArea

Parking Area refers to some area that enables parking of Vehicles. A Parking Area may contain sub-Parking Areas, the area of which may change. A Parking Area has some Parking Policy A Parking Area has some owner. A Parking Area may provide car changing stations. A Parking Area has some Location. A Parking Area may be occupied by some Vehicle (however, it might also be occupied by some debris or activities such as construction). There are different types (subclasses) of Parking Area, such as Street Parking Area, Lot Parking Area, Garage Parking Area, Illegal Parking Area, Loading/Unloading Zone Parking Area, Accessibility Parking Area
